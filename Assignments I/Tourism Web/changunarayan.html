Changu Narayan

HISTORY

Changu Narayan is an ancient Hindu temple located on a hilltop in the Kathmandu Valley of Nepal. This UNESCO World Heritage Site is dedicated to Lord Vishnu and holds immense historical and religious significance, believed to be one of the oldest temples in Nepal.

Description

The temple complex showcases intricate stone and wood carvings that depict Hindu deities, mythological stories, and artistic brilliance of the Licchavi period. The serene surroundings offer panoramic views of the valley and the Himalayas, creating a peaceful ambiance for spiritual contemplation and architectural appreciation.
