Dakshinkali

HISTORY

Dakshinkali Temple, situated in the Kathmandu Valley, is a revered Hindu shrine dedicated to the goddess Kali. It holds deep cultural and religious significance among locals and pilgrims.

Description

The temple is known for its rituals and sacrifices, where devotees offer animal sacrifices to the goddess Kali as part of religious traditions. Apart from the religious aspect, the temple's location amidst nature offers a serene escape for spiritual contemplation and scenic views of the surrounding hills.
