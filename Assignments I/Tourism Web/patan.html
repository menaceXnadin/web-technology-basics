Patan Durbar Square

HISTORY

Patan Durbar Square is a historic palace complex situated in the city of Patan, also known as Lalitpur, near Kathmandu. This UNESCO World Heritage Site showcases exquisite Newari architecture and culture, dating back to medieval times.

Description

The square is adorned with ancient palaces, temples, shrines, and courtyards, each narrating a tale of artistic brilliance and cultural richness. Intricately carved wooden structures, stone sculptures, and pagoda-style temples captivate visitors with their detailed craftsmanship. The Patan Museum within the square exhibits a diverse collection of art and artifacts, offering insights into the region's history and heritage.
