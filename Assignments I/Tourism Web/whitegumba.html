White Gumba

HISTORY

White Gumba, officially known as Seto Gumba, is a prominent Buddhist monastery situated on a hilltop overlooking the Kathmandu Valley. This serene and picturesque monastery holds cultural importance and offers panoramic views of the cityscape.

Description

The monastery features a white stupa, prayer wheels, and colorful flags amidst lush greenery, providing a tranquil escape from the city's hustle. Visitors can experience Tibetan Buddhist architecture, engage in meditation practices, and relish the peaceful ambiance while enjoying breathtaking vistas of Kathmandu and its surrounding hills.
