Mathematics

The Bachelor in Mathematics is a 4-year (semester-based) course focusing on advanced mathematical principles and applications. It is designed to equip students with comprehensive knowledge in various branches of mathematics, including calculus, algebra, statistics, and more. In addition to core mathematics courses, the program incorporates elective courses to broaden students' understanding and application of mathematical theories in different fields.

Objectives

The primary objective of the Mathematics program is to provide students with a strong foundation in mathematical theories, problem-solving skills, and analytical thinking. Graduates are expected to apply their mathematical knowledge in diverse fields such as finance, engineering, technology, and research.
