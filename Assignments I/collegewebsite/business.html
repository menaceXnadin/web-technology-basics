Business Studies

The Bachelor in Business Studies is a comprehensive 4-year program that offers a blend of theoretical knowledge and practical skills essential for understanding various aspects of the business world. It covers subjects like marketing, finance, management, and more to prepare students for a career in diverse business sectors.

Objectives

The primary objective of the Business Studies program is to equip students with a strong foundation in business principles, critical thinking, decision-making, and communication skills necessary to thrive in the competitive business environment.
