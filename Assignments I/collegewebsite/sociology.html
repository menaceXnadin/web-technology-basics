Sociology

A Bachelor of Arts in Sociology or BA in Sociology is a four-year program offered by Tribhuvan University. It provides knowledge in the areas of sociology and anthropology, focusing on the analysis of social and cultural institutions and offering a broader perspective that incorporates both global and local viewpoints.

Objectives

Bachelor of Arts in Sociology program aims to impart up-to-date knowledge on various branches of sociology through primary fieldwork training, secondary data analysis, and fact-finding techniques. It covers different areas of Nepalese society and culture, allowing students to gain a comprehensive understanding. Additionally, this Bachelor program focuses on developing friendly skills that contribute to overall social improvement. By studying this program, students can acquire valuable insights into the different branches of sociology, engage in practical fieldwork, analyze secondary data, and conduct fact-finding exercises. They will also gain a deep understanding of various aspects of Nepalese society and culture while honing their outgoing skills to drive positive change.
