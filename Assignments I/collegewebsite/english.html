Bachelor in English Studies

The Bachelor in English Studies program focuses on literature, culture, and language. It involves studying and analyzing English texts, with emphasis on literature from various English-speaking countries. Students explore contemporary and historical literary texts, language, literary studies, linguistics, and cultural studies. The program also covers rhetoric, composition, and writing. Graduates develop skills in creativity, analysis, and communication, enhancing their career prospects in teaching, writing, journalism, publishing, marketing, and more.
