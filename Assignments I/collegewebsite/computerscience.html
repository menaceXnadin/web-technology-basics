Computer Science

Course Overview: Computer Science of Tribhuvan University is a detailed and intensive program aiming to equip students with theoretical and practical knowledge in Information Technology and Computing. Spanning across a duration of four years and eight semesters, it encompasses a comprehensive 126 credit hour curriculum. For the interested candidates, Patan Multiple Campus offers a total of 144 seats, which includes 115 open seats and 29 reserved for the inclusive quota.

Objectives

  1. To provide intensive knowledge in theory, design, programming, and application of computers.
  2. To develop an in-depth understanding of and hands-on experience with computer systems.
  3. To foster creative and analytical skills for problem-solving in technology.
  4. To equip students with technical know-how essential for IT professionals.
