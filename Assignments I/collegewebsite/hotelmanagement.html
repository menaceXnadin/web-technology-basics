Hotel Management

The Bachelor's in Hotel Management is a comprehensive 4-year program designed to provide students with knowledge and skills related to the hospitality industry. This program covers various aspects of hotel operations, guest services, event management, and more, preparing students for diverse roles in the hospitality sector.

Objectives

The primary objective of the Hotel Management program is to equip students with the necessary expertise in managing hotel operations, guest relations, event planning, and other crucial areas within the hospitality industry.
